## How to Purchase

1.  **Connect Wallet:** Use a compatible Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom).
2.  **Select Tier:** Choose an available pricing tier.
3.  **Approve Transaction:** Pay in SOL or USDC at the current market rate.
4.  **License Minting:** Upon successful payment, a Metaplex Core NFT representing your license will be minted directly to your wallet.

---

## Activation & Rewards

-   **Software Download:** License holders can download the open-source node runner from the repository.
-   **Staking Required:** 10,000 $GRID must be bonded to the license NFT to start earning emissions.
-   **Reward Pool:** 10% of the total $GRID supply is allocated to the Initial Guardian Reward Pool.
